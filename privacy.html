Overview

DuoRig is designed as a local camera app. The app does not require an account, does not run ads, and does not use tracking or third-party analytics.

Data we collect

We do not collect personal data from the app. Camera, microphone, and media access are used on your device to create photos and videos.

Camera, microphone, and photo library

  • Camera access is used to preview and record dual-camera content.
  • Microphone access is used only when recording video with audio.
  • Photo library access is used only when you choose to save captured photos or videos.
  • Sharing is handled by the iOS share sheet and depends on the destination you choose.

Purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. DuoRig may receive purchase status from StoreKit to unlock access, but we do not receive your payment card details.

Support emails

If you contact us directly, we receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address and message content, only to respond to your request.

Children

DuoRig is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app or legal requirements change. The latest version will be available on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@freedomtecnologia.com.br.
